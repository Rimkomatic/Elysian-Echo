A Solitary Visitor

May 15, 2024
|
Love

In the first light’s tender embrace,
A solitary visitor found their place.
Through my window, with morning’s grace,
In sun’s pale hue, they revealed their face.

We sipped on dewdrops, silent and still,
As dawn’s embrace the world did fill.
In her promise, life widened its scope,
A realm where doubts and fears elope.

“I am the hunter of shadows deep,
With me, darkness fades, unable to creep.
In dreams, your life I’ll artfully craft.”
In love you softly laughed.

In whispers of laughter, dreams took flight,
With butterfly ink, I painted our night.
Your laughter, a muse, in hues profound,
As butterflies danced in my stomach, unbound.

In the stillness of the night’s embrace,
A solitary visitor filled the space.
“In the quiet need of not needing a thing,
Eyes beseeching, wordless, they sing,
In their silent whisper, ‘Hold me,’ awoken,
A longing unspoken, yet tenderly spoken.”

“In my tender embrace, I held you near,
Promising to linger, calming every fear.
With whispered vows, I sought to stall,
The rushing thoughts, as love enthralls.”

“If nightmares haunt your sleep’s gentle sway,
With kisses sweet, I’ll chase them away.
Each one a whisper of ancient love’s lore,
The forgotten kisses lovers did adore.
Reminding you, dear, I’m here to adore,
In our embrace, forevermore.”

“Why,” you whispered, a soft breeze’s plea,
“Is love as vast as the boundless sea?”
In that hushed moment, as our gazes entwine,
In the air, the scent of dreams divine.

With each exhale, a whispered sigh,
In your hair, the secrets of the sky.
With every glance, our souls ignite,
In the realm of love’s eternal light.
Amidst this dance of hearts, a tender wile,
Two smiles bloom, beneath love’s beguiling smile.
