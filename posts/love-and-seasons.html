Love and seasons

May 15, 2024
|
Love

on four different occasions I have been told that
sometimes love is just like the season;

like on the winter where we first held hands
during the walks we spent looking at the hope of a future together,
with messy minds and urgent hands,
we promised to stay.
where a fire started to break-out but we never cared –
said we’d burn together if we have to.

like the dried flower we shared
withering out in an envelope on a dresser stand during summer,
where late night talks existed,
as Persephone yearns for starlight and pomegranate,
where we watched the moon plummeting –
on tabooed lovers planting kisses at the wrong places.

like spring of contradiction, leaves budding,
but roots grew in tangled confusion of love and care
where our feelings bloom .

darling, we vowed never to relive the same
tragedy our pasts once wrote.
we vowed never to let our fire die out.
let's live the autumn everyday
Falling in love in the ways we're meant for fall for eachother deep
like those red leaf coloured in love
falling. falling. falling deeply with every second every moment.
